Personal Biography

About

Imani Niyigena is a web developer at Fathom Growth Solutions a business development start-up. As a systems development student he has been trained to perform highly in different IT areas such as Windows 10 OS, Microsoft Office 2013 & 2016, Windows store apps, SQL 2016 database development, web applications, mobile and device fundamentals, networking fundamentals and security fundamentals. During his time at Think360 and iStudent Academy, he worked closely with TechStable, a website and app development establishment.

His 2017 academic performance earned him the honour of being selected for the prestigious Microsoft Student to Business programme. From January 2018 to October 2018, he worked with certified Microsoft trainers on training certification candidates, designing and developing websites and this includes minor upgrades to the Think360 website. He has achieved other minor personal goals such as being an executive participant in an NPO and coming out second in the annual DevDay competition hosted by iStudent Academy in 2017.

He left Think360 and iStudent Academy with a long-term vision to gain experience through hard work and professional strategic planning. When he isn’t glued to a computer screen, he spends time enamoured by landscape and abstract art and photography; playing basketball for exercise, doing charity work, motivational speaking, writing, reading about science, religion, philosophy, politics and psychology; and perhaps most importantly to him, teaching himself machine learning.

Imani is currently working on a fictional novel about the philosophical implications of being constantly persuaded. His favourite quote is an excellent example of a very simple belief that conveys a lot of information about what he values most in his life: “Read the works of other men so you can reap what others have worked so hard for…”
